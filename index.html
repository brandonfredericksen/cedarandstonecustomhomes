Our Story

Building Dreams in the Heart of the Pacific Northwest

At Cedar and Stone, we believe your home should be a reflection of the stunning natural beauty that surrounds us. Our team of master craftsmen combines traditional building techniques with modern innovation to create homes that stand the test of time.

From the towering cedars to the rugged stone of our region, we source materials that honor the land and create spaces where families thrive for generations.
